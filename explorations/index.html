Technical Explorables

Sixteen interactive cuts through the one question the approaches left open — what is the computer actually doing while you play?

The approaches settled what the game is and forked on what it's made of. These explorables take that fork apart one piece at a time: each is a single technical aspect — a market that heals, a war you can memorize, a jumpgate web that hinges on one vertex, money that can run dry — built as a small live model you can poke, not just read. They were seeded by two design additions (a learnable broad-front enemy schedule; a jumpgate lane web) and an honest "does a utility AI even fit here?" audit, then fanned across the core simulations.

Every page is one self-contained file with a real, computed model behind it — drag a slider, cut a lane, drain a till, and the numbers actually move. Determinism throughout: same seed, same run.
