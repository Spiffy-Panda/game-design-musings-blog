 III · The application

The Ashfield Bout — a duel in seven exchanges →

Cinderwork against needlework under an invented dueling code (seals, ferns, no iron, bout to three). A slideshow chronicle: in-world commentary on every exchange, the marshals’ physics in the margin, and a tactical map per slide.

Reading order is the card order, but each page stands alone. Every page opens straight from disk — no server, no dependencies — and carries its own light and dark themes. Fictional physics throughout; the constants agree across all three pages.
