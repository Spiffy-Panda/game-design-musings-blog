Adventuring Guild Teller

Every adventuring town has one desk everything crosses: quests filed, proofs presented, ranks checked. This musing pitches the game where you are that desk — teller by day, town-shaper by night, and, if you want, author of the very characters whose paperwork you stamp.

Three parts, three lineages: a procedural checking loop built for flow (Papers, Please hands, Strange Horticulture materials), an after-hours guild floor where every real decision lives (Stardew's social half), and a townee fishbowl of autonomous characters you can author yourself and read about each morning (Tomodachi Life by way of Popup Dungeon).
